# Public Launch Hotfix Policy

## Allowed During Launch Week

- Crash/white screen fixes.
- Auth/signup/login fixes.
- Email verification fixes.
- Username onboarding fixes.
- Account deletion/privacy compliance fixes.
- Raw error mapping.
- App icon/label/store metadata fixes.
- Critical UI overflow blocking actions.
- Security/secrets cleanup.

## Not Allowed During Launch Week

- Push notifications.
- Apple Sign In unless required by review.
- Payments.
- Ads/monetization expansion.
- Major UI redesign.
- New chat architecture.
- Algorithmic feed.
- New admin/business features.
- AI features.
- Large refactors.

## Reason

Keep launch stable and avoid introducing new blockers.
