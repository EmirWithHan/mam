# Play Store Feature Graphic Plan

Date: 2026-06-06

## Goal

Create a clean promotional image for Match A Man that feels modern, social,
sporty, energetic, and trustworthy without making exaggerated claims.

## Concept

- Soft modern sports/social app feeling.
- Coral/salmon `#FF7E79` visible as a brand color.
- App logo/icon visible.
- Phone mockup optional.
- Sport/social activity cues such as field lines, ball shapes, activity cards,
  or event UI hints.
- No misleading dating imagery.
- No copyrighted photos.
- No real people without permission.

## Text Ideas

- Sporla sosyalleş
- Etkinlik oluştur, ekibini bul
- Maç, voleybol, yürüyüş ve daha fazlası

## Warnings

- Do not overcrowd text.
- Do not use small unreadable copy.
- Do not show features that do not exist.
- Do not imply a safety guarantee.
- Do not claim official status, ranking, or guaranteed results.
