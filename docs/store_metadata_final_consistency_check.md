# Store Metadata Final Consistency Check

Use this before production/App Store submission.

## Check Consistency Across

- App name.
- Category.
- Short description/subtitle.
- Full description.
- Screenshots.
- Privacy policy.
- Account deletion.
- Data Safety/App Privacy.
- Reviewer notes.
- Beta/release notes.
- Support email/URL.

## Warnings

- Do not claim dating-only purpose.
- Do not claim guaranteed safety.
- Do not exaggerate moderation.
- Do not show private tester data in screenshots.
- Do not mention unsupported features like push notifications if not
  implemented.
- Do not mention Apple Sign In unless implemented.
- Do not imply Match A Man organizes, supervises, guarantees, insures, or
  physically controls user-created events.
