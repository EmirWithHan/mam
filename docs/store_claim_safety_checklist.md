# Store Claim Safety Checklist

Date: 2026-06-06

Use this checklist before copying text into Play Console or store graphics.

## Allowed Claims

- Users can create events.
- Users can join events.
- Users can share posts.
- Users can search profiles.
- Users can send feedback/report.
- Business accounts can apply if implemented in the submitted build.

## Risky Claims

- "Verified users" unless verification exists and is clearly defined.
- "Safe events" as a guarantee.
- "Best app".
- "Official".
- "Guaranteed".
- "Real-time" unless in-app realtime is stable in the submitted build.
- "Free forever".
- "No ads ever".
- "Anonymous" unless true.
- "Secure" without context.

## Required Final Checks

- Listing text matches actual app behavior.
- Privacy policy matches Data Safety answers.
- Screenshots show real implemented features.
- Legal/account deletion docs are aligned.
- No fake ranking, pricing, or promo claims.
- No dating-app positioning or wording.
- No claim that every user, event, or business is verified unless actually true.
