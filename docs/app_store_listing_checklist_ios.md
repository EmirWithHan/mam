# iOS App Store Listing Checklist

Use this checklist when creating the App Store Connect listing for Match A Man.
The iOS listing should stay aligned with the Google Play listing and should not
position the app as a dating app.

## Required Metadata

- App name: Match A Man
- Subtitle
- Promotional text
- Description
- Keywords
- Support URL
- Marketing URL, if available
- Privacy Policy URL
- Account deletion URL or clear deletion support URL
- Category
- Age rating
- Copyright
- Screenshots
- App icon
- Review notes
- Reviewer test account
- Contact info

## Listing Guardrails

- Describe Match A Man as a social sports and events app.
- Do not position it as a dating app.
- Do not claim the app is 100% safe.
- Do not claim official verification, insurance, supervision, or guaranteed
  event safety unless those systems actually exist.
- Do not use fake screenshots.
- Do not show real private user data in screenshots.
- Keep legal, privacy, and account deletion links consistent with the live app.

## Turkish-First Alignment

The in-app MVP copy is Turkish-first. Store copy can be localized separately,
but Turkish screenshots and Turkish user flows should be ready before Turkish
beta or public launch.
