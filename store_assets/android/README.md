# Android Store Assets

This folder is for Google Play Store visual assets for Match A Man.

## Folders

- `screenshots/`: final exported phone screenshots for Play Console.
- `feature_graphic/`: final exported feature graphic assets.
- `icon/`: final exported Play Store icon assets.

## Rules

- Do not store private user photos.
- Do not store real personal phone numbers, emails, or addresses in screenshots.
- Do not store source design files with licensed assets unless the license
  allows it.
- Prefer final exported PNG/JPEG assets when ready.
- Do not commit huge raw captures unless intentionally needed.
- Do not use unrelated business branding.
- Do not create fake screenshots that show unavailable features.
